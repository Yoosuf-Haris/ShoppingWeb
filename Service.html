Specialist Consultation




Our Specialist Consultation service is trusted by many, for providing access to the top medical specialists in the country at affordable rates. The service comprises all major specialties as well as a large number of sub-specialties, while drawing on the services of a group of over 350 highly-experienced specialist consultants from the leading hospitals across the country. By ensuring a large number of the country's top specialists practice at our hospitals, we afford greater access to top-notch healthcare in generally underserved markets.




Pharmacy




Arogya maintains a number of hospital-attached pharmacies in several key locations across the island. Our pharmacies are a one-stop shop for you to purchase all your pharmaceutical and medical consumable needs. Our pharmacy staff are friendly and maintain impeccable service standards, while being guided and supervised by a team of qualified and experienced pharmacists. Arogya's pharmacy chain offers a wide range of medicine and other medical plus surgical consumables,listed in both generic and trade names for your convenience.




Operation Theatre




Arogya operates 2 state-of-the-art surgical theaters, offering our customers the ability to undergo surgery within a private healthcare setting. We cover a wide range of both major and minor surgeries under general, spinal, and local anesthesia. Highly specialized surgery packages are offered to our customers to ensure they realize the best value for money. Additionally, we are registered with all major insurance companies and facilitate the maintenance of corporate medical schemes to reimburse healthcare and surgery-related expenses.




Radiology Department




The Medihelp Radiology Department is an integral part of our Medical Imaging services. The Medihelp Hospital Horana, Medihelp Matugama, Medihelp Beruwala, Medihelp Piliyandala, and Medihelp Moratuwa X-ray units are equipped with state-of-the-art Computed Radiographic Systems. Our highly versatile digitizers allow for the production of ultra-clear radiographic images. A distinct advantage of the ultra-modern systems we utilize is that they ensure the film is available within a period of 2 minutes, while maintaining great accuracy and image clarity.
